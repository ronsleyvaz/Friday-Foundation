# Show Your Friday

A gallery for founders who have built something with Friday Foundation. Add your entry and open a pull request.

---

## How to add your entry

Copy the template below, fill it in, and add it to the bottom of this file. Keep it short. One screen per entry.

```
### [Your name or handle] - [What you built]

**Business:** [One line on what you do]

**Commands running:** [Which commands from the pack you use]

**What it changed:** [One or two sentences. The concrete before and after.]

**Friday folder:** [A line or two describing what your friday/ config looks like]

**One thing you built on top:** [If you wrote a custom command or agent, describe it in one sentence]
```
